Privacy Policy for SalahFlow

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

SalahFlow (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a Muslim prayer application designed to help users access location-based prayer timings, learn how to pray, read the complete Quran with audio, and view Islamic news content.

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the SalahFlow mobile application (“App”).

If you have any questions, contact us at:
cloudloftcreatives@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Location Information (Approximate / Precise Location)

We request your location only to show accurate prayer timings based on your location.
Your location is:

Not stored permanently

Not shared with third parties

Not used for any purpose other than calculating prayer times

You may disable location at any time, but some features may not function accurately.

1.2 Device Information

We may collect basic, non-personal information such as:

Device type

Operating system version

App version

Anonymous usage data

This helps us improve app performance and stability.

1.3 No Personal Data is Required

SalahFlow does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal identifiable information such as:

Name

Email

Phone number

Address

You use the app anonymously.

2. Quran, Audio, and News Content
2.1 Quran Reading & Audio

For Quran reading and audio playback:

Audio and translation files may be loaded from trusted servers or public Quran repositories.

No personal information is transmitted during playback.

2.2 Islamic News Feed

News content is fetched from publicly available Islamic news sources.

We do not track your news reading behavior or personalize content.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information collected to:

Display accurate prayer timings

Improve app performance

Fix bugs and enhance stability

Ensure Quran, audio, and news content loads properly

We do not:

Sell your data

Share your data with advertisers

Track you across other websites or apps

4. Third-Party Services

SalahFlow may use third-party tools for:

Anonymous analytics

Quran audio hosting/CDN

Islamic news feeds

These services do not receive any personal identifiable information from us.

5. Children’s Privacy

SalahFlow is safe for all ages, including children.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal data accidentally, contact us at cloudloftcreatives@gmail.com
 and we will remove it immediately.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect all data processed in the app.
Because we do not store personal identifiable data, risk remains minimal.

7. Your Choices

You may:

Disable location access at any time

Delete the app to stop all data processing

Control app permissions from device settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any updates will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date above.

Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
